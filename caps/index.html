About us

Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Marikina Senior High School (PLMAR SHS) is dedicated to providing quality education that prepares students for success in their chosen fields. We offer a variety of academic tracks, including STEM, ABM, and HUMSS, that help students build the skills they need for their future careers. Our school also encourages personal growth through extracurricular activities, leadership opportunities, and community involvement. With a supportive team of teachers and a welcoming environment, we strive to help every student reach their full potential, develop strong values, and become responsible and engaged citizens. At PLMAR SHS, we believe in creating well-rounded individuals ready to face the challenges of the future.
